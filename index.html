I'm the founder and President of Open Heart + Mind (OHM), a charity project using gift-based festivals to prototype new models for society. My main focus is WISDOM, a generalizable open review framework designed from first principles to revolutionize the way communities recognize and reward contributions. Read more about my projects here.

My journey to this point began in architecture, pivoted at a meditation retreat, and passed through a neuroscience PhD and out the other side when I realised that the game of academia is broken. Since then, I've been playing a different kind of game to fund my dream of a fair and open research system, where everyone gets to play. Read more about my journey here.
