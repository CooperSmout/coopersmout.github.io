I'm the founder and President of Open Heart + Mind (OHM), a charity project using gift-based festivals to prototype new models for society. My main focus is WISDOM, a generalizable open review framework designed from first principles to revolutionize the way communities recognize and reward contributions. Read more about my projects here.

My journey to this point has been long and winding, beginning with architecture, pivoting at a meditation retreat, and passing through a PhD and out the other side when I realised that the game of academia is broken. Since then, I've been playing poker to fund my new dream of a fair and open research system where everyone gets to play. Read more about my journey here. Download my resume here.
